Furniture Give-Away Registration Acknowledgement

Dear Furniture Give-Away participant:

Thanks for pre-registering to attend the International Furniture Give-Away on Saturday, August 19, 2017.

A confirmation email with a record of all the information you submitted has been sent to the email address you provided. If you do not receive the confirmation message within a few minutes of signing up, please check your Junk E-mail folder just in case the email got delivered there instead of your inbox. We recommend that you add elan@ifmusa.org to your 'Approved Sender' list, within your email reader.

Please check to see that all the information submitted above are accurate. If not email Elan Rajamani (elan@ifmusa.org) with the corrections right away.

Free transportation will be provided for those who need it. Pickup time and location will be emailed to you a few days before the event.

Your specific appointment time will be announced closer to the event. Please show up at the apropriate time at your pickup location. Do not come to the event earlier. You will be served at your appointment time.

The International Furniture Give-Away event is especially arranged for NEW international students, scholars and their families. If you are a returning student and need furniture, please contact us after August 19 and we will try our best to secure furniture for you. Also, you can find furniture and other items at very low cost at the Hoosier to Hoosier to Hoosier Community Sale on Aug 19 beginning at 8:00am.

Pre-registration is required to attend this event. Encourage your friends to pre-register if they are planning to attend the event. Must pre-register by Thursday, August 10.

Please note: All pre-registered students will be given a specific appointment time to arrive and will be served according to that appointment time. Students are discouraged from coming earlier than their appointment time. Those coming earlier have to wait till their appointment time.

On arrival, student will go through a registration process. The order in which students gets to choose furniture has already been decided based on where each person lives. Typically, a student might be able to choose 2 to 3 furniture items and bag full of household/kitchen items. Refreshments & snacks will be provided throughout the event. Students are required to return to their apartment immediately after choosing their furniture and picking up household/kitchen items. Plan on being at your apartment until 6pm on the day of the event to take delivery of your furniture items. Most students will receive their furniture much earlier.

It is important that you provide accurate address and phone number so that we can deliver the items in a timely manner. If any of your information changes after you had registered online, please email Elan Rajamani at with the changes.

The map and directions to Sherwood Oaks Christian Church is below

Directions from IU Campus: Go south on Jordan to Atwater (stop light). Turn left. Go east on Atwater. Turn right onto High Street (south). Go south on High Street. Pass Child’s Elementary School on left. Go ¾ the way around the Roundabout and take a right onto E. Rogers Road. The church is at the top of the hill on the right, 2700 E. Rogers Road. Enter through the South Entrance

Please contact Elan Rajamani at elan@ifmusa.org or 812-325-9047 if you have questions.

We look forward to seeing you at the International Furniture Give-Away.

BISM Staff
