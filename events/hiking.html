Hiking Trip to McCormick's Creek State Park

Come enjoy Nature,Make New Freinds,Learn More the American State Park on this Holiday



Join us as we hike through the beautiful hills and forests of this local Indiana state park. We will hike some, eat picnic meal and play some games and enjoy the nature.

We will be going to McCormick's Creek State Park which is the oldest state park in the U.S. state of Indiana.

On arriving ,we will be hiking through the beautiful trails, eating some tasty picnic food, playing some games.

Picnic lunch will be provided (vegetarian meals are also available). You are welcome to bring your own snacks as well :)

This is a FREE Event and OPEN TO ALL .

All Internationals and Americans Welcome.
