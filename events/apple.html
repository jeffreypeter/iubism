Apple Picking Trip

A chance to visit an apple orchard where you can pick, buy, and eat apples.



Come spend the afternoon tasting a variety of apples for free and purchase your choice of apples, pumpkins, and other fall fruits and vegetables. Also, enjoy the fall festival and craft fair held right at the orchard.
Where are we Going?
We will be going to Anderson Orchard which is one of a handful of orchards in Indiana that offers the unique experience of picking your own produce! U-Pick Produce at Anderson Orchard in Mooresville, Indiana Red Raspberries, Blue Plums, Pumpkins, Apples, and Chestnuts are available throughout the season.
What do I need to carry with me ?
You will need to bring enough money for buying any apples you want (~$18.00/large bag), lunch (~$5 - $7), and any souvenirs.
Where must I go to for the pickup?
Meet us at the front entrance of Eigenmann Hall (corner of 10th St and Union St) at 10am for transportation
