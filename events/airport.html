Airport Pickup Service

We assist students arriving for IU’s International Orientation from the Indianapolis Airport (IND) to the campus of Indiana University-Bloomington between July 26 - August 9, 2017.

BISM staff and volunteers will meet new internationals at the baggage claim area at 3:00pm and 11:00pm daily at the Indianapolis Airport and take you by car or van directly to your IU campus or off-campus apartment or to a Bloomington area hotel as you have arranged.

FAQ

How Do I Request a Free Ride?
To request a ride, please complete the BISM Airport Pickup Request form.  We will pick up registered students at 3:00pm or 11:00pm each day. The ride to Bloomington will take approximately one hour.
Where must I go to for the pickup?
Meeting location for the airport pickup is at the baggage claim area of the airport.
What do I do if I miss the pickup?
If you miss the 3:00pm pickup, you can wait for the 11:00pm pickup, or you can take the shuttle service offered at the airport. Go Express Travel offers low cost shuttle service to Bloomington every 2 hours from 6:40am to 10:40pm. If you miss the 11:00pm pickup, you should plan for alternative mode of transportation. Taxi service is available at the airport.
How much does the airport pickup service cost?
The airport pickup service is offered as a free service by the staff and volunteers of Bloomington International Student Ministries (BISM) and does not cost you anything. Visit www.iubism.org/about.htm to learn more about BISM and the services that we provide international students from IU.
