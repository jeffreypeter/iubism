International Welcome Banquet

Let's celebrate the start of your school year together.

Join us for an evening of free food, fun, games, music and activities to network with internationals and Americans alike.

During the banquet, get an introduction to the US and helpful tips navigating campus and American culture.





We will be having local homemade American Food.

This is a FREE Event and Open to all Internationals.
