13th International
Furniture Give-Away
for New International Students of Indiana University

Free Furniture and household items for new international students

Yes! We’re giving away free furniture, household & kitchen items to NEW international students, scholars, and families of all national and religious backgrounds. 

Come and choose from a wide variety of  gently used items.



What items are available
at the Give-Away?
Here is a list of some of the items you might find:
Beds - twin, double, queen
Study desks, chairs, couches, book shelves, dining tables, chest of drawers, dorm refrigerators, entertainment centers, side tables, dressers, kitchen/household items and more.



Details

  • August 19

  • 8 :15 am

  • South Entrance
    Sherwood Oaks Christian Church 2700 E. Rogers Rd (corner of E. Rogers & Sare) Bloomington, IN 47401

How do I get to select my furniture?
On arrival, students will go through a registration process. The order in which students gets to choose furniture has already been decided based on where each person lives. Typically, a student might be able to choose 2 to 3 furniture items and bag full of household/kitchen items. Plan on being at the event for 1 to 3 hours. Most students will be done much faster. Refreshments & snacks will be provided throughout the event.



Will my furniture be delivered to my apartment?
Yes, all furniture & larger household items will be delivered to your home. It is important that you provide accurate address and phone number so that we can deliver the items in a timely manner. Plan on being at your apartment until 6pm to take delivery of your furniture items.


Is there a cost to this service?
The Furniture Give-Away is offered as a free service by the staff and volunteers of Bloomington International Student Ministries (BISM) and churches in the Bloomington area. It does not cost you anything. Visit www.iubism.org/about.htm to learn more about BISM and the services that we provide international students from IU.


How can I pre-register to attend this event?
All NEW international students must pre-register by Thursday, August 10 to attend the International Furniture Give-Away.

 


How can I get to the event?
Free transportation is provided for those who need it from various locations in town. Pickup locations and time will be announced closer to the event date.

If you plan to drive to the event, you need to arrive at times that will be announced through email. Please follow the following directions:

Map & Directions to Sherwood Oaks Christian Church

Directions from IU Campus: Go south on Jordan to Atwater (stop light). Turn left. Go east on Atwater. Turn right onto High Street (south). Go south on High Street. Pass Child’s Elementary School on left. Go ¾ the way around the Roundabout and take a right onto E. Rogers Road. The church is at the top of the hill on the right, 2700 E. Rogers Road. Enter through the South Entrance
 
I am a returning international student. Can I attend the Furniture Give-Away?
The International Furniture Give-Away event is especially arranged for NEW international students, scholars and their families. If you are a returning student and need furniture, please contact us after August 19 and we will try our best to secure furniture for you. Also, you can find furniture and other items at very low cost at the Hoosier to Hoosier Community Sale on Aug 19 (8am - 3pm).
