Spotlight on Summer (SOS) 

Spotlight on Summer (SOS)

is an opportunity to meet in a local American home for a relaxing time of food, friendship, and discussion of short films that explore spiritual questions.

At SOS we will watch a six-part video series called God Provides"

Throughout history, people have wrestled with the concept of trusting God to meet their needs versus relying on their own efforts, other people, money, or other things society has to offer.

Thankfully, through stirring accounts and parables found in the Bible, God reveals how He wants to be our Provider above anything else. These relevant stories present us wisdom, truth, encouragement and practical steps we can take to develop and deepen a relationship of trust with the God of the universe.

This six-part video series is helpful for people of all cultural and religious backgrounds.

Week 1: Widow and Oil
What do you have?
God has given us all something, and He wants us to be faithful with whatever we have.  

Week 2: Jeremiah’s Call
God’s Design for Hope
This poignant story shows us God’s part and our part in His plans for our future—even in very challenging circumstances.  

Week 3: Abram’s Reward
Making Major Life Choices
God is near to those who are faithful, even with things as small as money and possessions.

Week 4: Abraham and Isaac
Mine or His?
God teaches Abraham that obeying Him is better than holding tightly to any of His gifts or blessings.

Week 5: Rich Man and Lazarus
My Heart Decisions Affect Eternity
This story dramatically illustrates God’s love for the poor and His rebuke for the selfish and prideful.

Week 6: Lifted Up
My Hope for the Future
This powerful portrayal of the gospel of Jesus Christ uniquely connects the 1400-year span between the time of Moses and the ministry of Jesus.
