Walmart Shopping Trip

We provide transportation to Wal-Mart Superstore so that you can shop for household items and groceries at discount prices.



Join us as we provide transportation to Walamrt Super Center where you can shop for household items and groceries at discount prices.

We will be providing transportation on 3 days between August 7th to August 10th.

Monday, August 7, 3-6pm
Wednesday, August 9, 7-10pm
Thursday, August 10, 3-6pm

Meet at the front entrance of Eigenmann Hall (corner of 10th St and Union St) for transportation.

On arriving at the SuperStore,students will be able to shop for 3 hours after which they will be dropped back to their apartments.

Register to come on Wednesday if you are planning to purchase big items like bicycle or other items that cannot fit into a van. We'll have a trailer on this day only.

This is a FREE Event and OPEN TO ALL .

Hurry Up and Register before seats get filled up .
