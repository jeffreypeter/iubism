Information Update Confirmation
 

Hi,

Thanks for updating your information and indicating whether you are coming to the International Furniture Give-Away on Saturday, August 19, 2017.

If you are coming, we look forward to seeing you on Saturday. For any reason, if you are not able to attend the event, please email elan@ifmusa.org to let me know that you will not be attending.

You would be receiving another email on Thursday or Friday indicating your appointment time for the event. That is the time you are supposed to show up at Sherwood Oaks Christian Church. For those who requested for rides, your pickup location and pickup times will also be indicated. All pickups, furniture and delivery of furniture is FREE. We will deliver all your furniture to your apartment.

Please remember that the International Furniture Give-Away event is especially arranged for NEW international students, scholars and their families. If you are a returning student who first arrived in Bloomington in 2016 or earlier, you are not eligible to participate in this event. Please help the maximum number of our new students to benefit from this event by not participating. If returning students need furniture, please contact us after August 19 and we will try our best to secure furniture for you. Also, you can find furniture and other items at very low cost at the Hoosier to Hoosier Community Sale on Aug 19 beginning at 8:00am.

Please contact Elan Rajamani at elan@ifmusa.org or 812-325-9047 if you have questions.

We look forward to seeing you at the International Furniture Give-Away.

 

BISM Staff
