About Us

BISM provides services and activities that encourage all internationals at IU to develop friendships, enjoy American culture, and, if interested, grow in understanding of the Christian faith. Students from all countries and religious backgrounds are welcome to participate in any activities that interest them, and all services are offered in an atmosphere of friendship and respect.
